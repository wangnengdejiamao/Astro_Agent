#### 2. TRIPLE DIRECTION MUST RESPECT GRAMMATICAL LOGIC

- In passive voice (**"X was [verb]ed by Y"**, **"X measured using Y"**):
  - X = logical object (e.g., property, sample)
  - Y = agent/instrument/material/method
  - Triple: `[X, relation, Y]`

  ✅ Correct:  
  `["polysulfide concentration", "is_measured_by", "UV-vis spectroscopy"]`  
  ❌ Wrong:  
  `["UV-vis spectroscopy", "is_measured_by", "polysulfide concentration"]`

- For active voice: follow subject–verb–object order.

**Examples**:
- Active: "Li2S nucleates on carbon surface" → `["Li2S", "nucleates_on", "carbon"]`
- Passive: "Polysulfides were adsorbed by CNT" → `["polysulfides", "are_adsorbed_by", "CNT"]`
- Active: "CoNiCuMnMg@C catalyst boosts Li ion diffusion" → `["CoNiCuMnMg@C", "boosts", "Li ion diffusion"]`
