### SEMANTIC

- **Relation types**: Use **only** the following key relations to judge whether two triples are semantically related. Do NOT invent new relation labels:

  - `supports`: one triple provides evidence or supports the other.
  - `contradicts`: one triple contradicts the other.
  - `elaborates`: one triple elaborates or refines the other (e.g., same concept, different detail).

  - `enables`: one triple enables or facilitates the effect described in the other.
- **Important**: Only output pairs that have a meaningful relationship. Do NOT include pairs with no clear relationship.

- **Hint – discourse connectives**: Pay attention to connectives such as *because*, *thus*, *therefore*, *which*, *leading to*.
